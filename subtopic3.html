Abstract This article explains how the digital divide affects student learning by highlighting the unequal access to devices and internet. Source 2

URL American Psychological Association
Author American Psychological Association
Abstract This APA article explores how excessive screen time impacts student well-being, including sleep, focus, and stress levels. It explains that multitasking on digital devices can weaken learning by dividing attention. Although technology has benefits, the article warns that too much screen time may harm academic performance and mental health. The APA recommends tech-free breaks, structured device use, and mindful habits to help students maintain balance. This source provides important insight into the challenges of heavy technology use in education.
