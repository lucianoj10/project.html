Abstract This Edutopia article explains how technology enhances students' learning by increasing engagement and providing multiple ways to understand content. Author UNESCO
Abstract UNESCO describes how educational technology supports inclusive learning by providing resources for students with diverse needs. Tools like text-to-speech, captioning, and translation features make learning more accessible. The article emphasizes how digital learning platforms help close the equity gap by giving students access to high-quality resources regardless of location. Technology also supports personalized learning, letting students progress at their own pace. Overall, UNESCO presents technology as a powerful way to support fairness, access, and equal opportunities in education.
